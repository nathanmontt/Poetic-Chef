poetic chef

A new way to read food.

the concept

We, as a team, were trying to look to something that catch people eyes. So we came up with the idea of a blog that states a different point of view about food, not the ones that are consider, by the others, as an "elite", but the daily food, the food that we all know about: our comforting food, the ones that makes us remember our childhood, the ones that we love to eat with our friends, and those that we need to eat by ourselves. We, as a team, value this type of things. We care about these little things, and that's why Poetic Chef was created: to remember who you are, with food.
